About the shop

Hand knitted merino designer baby clothing made in New Zealand using local materials.

Soft and snug for your baby's delicate skin our garments are perfect all year round, keeping little ones warm in winter and cool in summer.

A range of clothing and accessories for premature babies through to 4yrs.
